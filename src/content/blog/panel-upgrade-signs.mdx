If your panel is warm, crowded with tandem breakers, or under 200A for a high-demand home, reliability and safety can degrade quickly.

## Typical indicators

1. Breaker trips during normal appliance use.
2. Remodeling plans requiring new dedicated circuits.
3. Insurance carrier concern over obsolete panel brands.
4. EV, heat pump, or generator additions on the roadmap.
